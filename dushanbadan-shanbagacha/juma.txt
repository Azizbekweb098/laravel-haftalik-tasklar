Juma: Queue va Jobs
Maqsad: Laravel queue tizimi orqali katta vazifalarni avtomatlashtirish.

O‘rganish:
Queue va Jobs nima uchun kerak.
Redis yoki database driver’ni sozlash.
Jobs’ni bajarish va monitoring qilish.
Amaliyot:

Foydalanuvchilar soni ko‘p bo‘lganda email yuborish vazifasini queue yordamida bajaring.
Failed jobs bilan ishlashni o‘rganing.
