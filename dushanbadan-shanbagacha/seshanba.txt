Seshanba: Events va Listeners
Maqsad: Laravel Event tizimini tushunish va foydalanishni o‘rganish.

O‘rganish:
Event-Listener nima va qanday ishlaydi.
Event va Listener yaratish (php artisan make:event va make:listener).
Event’larni queue yordamida ishlatish.
Amaliyot:

Foydalanuvchi ro‘yxatdan o‘tganida "Welcome" email yuboruvchi Event va Listener yarating.
Log yozishni Event orqali avtomatlashtiring.
